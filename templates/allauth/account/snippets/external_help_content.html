fol.io empowers web developers with the ability to efficiently produce numerous portfolios (or "folios") fitting various job descriptions.

Through the use of folio snippets that are respectively linked to commonly found sections in portfolios, users can create, update and re-use these snippets within their folios to their hearts content.

This means that fol.io users are empowered with a rich collection of folios that all share various snippets, which can be updated, swapped and deleted in no time.
