A folio skill snippet represents an individual skill you posess. A skill snippet can only be one of two categories, the first being a tech skill, the last being a soft skill.

For each skill snippet, you can provide a title (being the name of the skill) and a description as to how you obtained that skill.

You can attach as many of these as you'd like to a folio, however it is of course recommended that you be selective and concise with what you put in front of a potential employer.
