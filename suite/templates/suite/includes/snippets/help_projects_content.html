A project snippet represents a project you would've made in the past. You can attach a picture, describe the project in depth and attach links so potential employers can check out your code/work.

Folios are all about being concise and to the point (which is in the interest of your potential employers of course). So with this in mind, a folio can only contain 4 project snippets, so choose your projects wisely!

NOTE: We strongly recommended that you provide a picture that has an aspect ratio of 16:9 (e.g., 1280x720, 1920x1080 etc.). Pictures with a different aspect ratio are very likely provide unfavourable results.

We will be looking to support various image aspect ratios in the future.
