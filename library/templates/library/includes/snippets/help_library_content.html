The library is where you manage high level functionality regarding your folios.

This functionality includes the creation of folios, editing their titles/descriptions, publishing/concealing them or deleting them entirely.

Folios are concealed by default when created. Assuming you have licenses available, you can publish a folio by clicking the Publish option within it's additional menu.

If you haven't bought any licenses yet, you can purchase some at the license store.

Legend

visibility  - folio is published.  The folio can be viewed by anyone.

visibility_off  - folio is concealed.  The folio can only be viewed by the author, in other words, you!
